Our Coffee

Since 1971, it always has been and will always be about quality. We’re passionate about ethically sourcing only the finest Arabica coffee beans and roasting them with great care. Our passion for coffee is rivaled only by our love of sharing it.

Planet-positive tips

April is earth month, but our commitment to reducing our environmental impact is year round. Try these tips on your next Kofi visit.

Coffea Arabica

Our most high-quality variety offers a smooth, complex flavor and distinct lack of bitterness.

Coffea Liberica

Introducing our new Coffea liberica is prized for its piquant floral aroma and bold, smoky flavor profile.

Coffea Excelsa

Light roast traits like tart notes and fruity flavors with flavors that are more reminiscent of dark roasts.
